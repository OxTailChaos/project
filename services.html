SERVICES


Music Production


Oxtail

Producing for hip-hop acts for over 10 years, Oxtail (Formerly BeatsByBAGS) is our in-house producer (as well as co-owner of Amnesia 11). With a wide array of sounds and styles at his arsenal, Oxtail still manages to have a signature style that's instantly recognizable.


J-Mist

Originally producing on the legendary Neko workstation, J-Mist's style isn't your typical jazzy boom-bap. Utilizing unique drump patterns and sequencing, his affinity for early 90's hip-hop is definitely an influence, but not the only trick he has up his sleeve.



Video Production

Shallow Thoughts, the music video direction/production arm of Amnesia 11, has been steadily gaining a name for themselves as a creative force to be wreckoned with. Over the past few years, they've pushed the envelope in terms of what an indie music video can look like, giving a big budget feel for a fraction of the price. Recording Studio

Located in upper manhattan, the Amnesia 11 Recording Studio is a smaller, affordable studio, most suited for solo vocalist recording. Our in-house engineers are equipped to handle vocalists of many genres, from Hip-Hop to Opera, to voice-overs.



A&R/Executive Production

With an immense love for music, and more specifically, the projects that contain them (albums), Syvaun Grullon was playlisting before it was a word. Often creating his own concept-based mixes, at some point his ear for what works, what doesn't, and how to sequence it all became evident. Along with long time collaborator Oxtail, they've Executive Produced and A&R'd multiple projects to see that the end product is finished with a level of polish and sophistication usually seen on much higher budget releases.
