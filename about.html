ABOUT

Concieved as a one-stop-shop for musicians, producers, and creatives alike, Amnesia 11 was launched in 2019 with the goal of streamlining the creative process for artists, and making these services available to different experience levels and budgets. We offer song production, recording studio options, music video production, and even A&R/executive producer services.
